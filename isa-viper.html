About This Yacht

ISA VIPER 120

The VIPER series by ISA is the next generation of sport yachts combining performance & styling and lifestyle into a single platform. The impressive aft deck features fold down balconies, pool and lounge areas into one. Alfresco dining is a common theme on both the main deck and upper sundeck. The VIPER 120 sleeps 10 guests in 5 cabins including an impressive full beam master en-suite, spacious VIP, and 3 other guest cabins. Tender garage complement the spaces and allows for ample toy storage. VIPER- the next generation of yachting realized today.
